Our Story

Loving Homes is a luxury hotel for dogs based in the Maldives. With rising demand for our premium services, our founders set out to build a safe, fun, and comforting environment for every dog that stays with us.

We believe dogs deserve more than just basic boarding‚Äîthey deserve a home away from home where joy, safety, and love are guaranteed. This belief is the foundation of everything we do at Loving Homes.

Every detail of our facility is designed with care, from our cozy suites to our enriching play areas and personalised services. Our dedicated team treats every dog like family, ensuring they feel relaxed, happy, and truly at home. Whether your dog is here for a short stay or a long vacation, we are committed to giving them a luxury experience filled with comfort, affection, and unforgettable moments.

Our Mission

We are committed to offering exceptional, modern pet care. Our mission includes:

  • Providing high-quality facilities like grooming parlours, luxury suites with heating/cooling, TVs, and outdoor paddocks.
  • Offering 24/7 veterinary access and attentive, trained caregivers.
  • Creating custom packages that cater to the unique needs of dogs and their owners.
  • Using technology to improve communication with clients through updates, photos, and messaging.
